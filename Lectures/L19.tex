\section{DFT}
DTFS, period $N$.
\\definitons:
\\$x[n] = \sum a_k \cdot e^{j2 \frac{\pi}{N}kn}
\\k \in \{0,1,..N-1\}
\\a_N = \frac{1}{N} \sum x[n] \cdot e^{-j \frac{2\pi}{N}kn}
\\n \in \{0,1,..N-1\}
\\$Example:
\\$x[n] = \sin{(\frac{2\pi}{5})} =
\frac{1}{2j}e^{j(\frac{2\pi}{5})n} -
\frac{1}{2j}e^{-j(\frac{2\pi}{5})n}
\\\therefore a_1 = \frac{1}{2j}, a_{-1} = -\frac{1}{2j}
\\a_{-1} = a_{4},
\\a_{1} = a_{5}
$
\\Plot the graph.
